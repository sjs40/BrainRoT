# BrainRoT Logo Spec

## Source Files
- `app-icon-light.svg`
- `app-icon-dark.svg`
- `logo-mark.svg`

## Brand Colors
- Brain blue: `#69B0F6`
- Brain blue deep: `#4E94EF`
- Brain orange: `#FF933F`
- Brain orange deep: `#FF8A32`
- Light container: `#F5F7FB`
- Dark container: `#10141C`
- Internal cut marks: `#FFFFFF`

## Safe Area
- Outer rounded-square container uses roughly 28px margin on a 1024px canvas.
- Brain mark sits centered with comfortable breathing room on all sides.
- Keep the vertical split aligned to center.

## Small-Size Guidance
- At `16px` and `32px`, preserve the core split-brain silhouette and central divider first.
- Internal white cut marks may need slight optical thickening in raster export if they close up.
- Do not alter the blue/orange split or rounded-square container.

## Usage Notes
- Use the light icon on light app/package surfaces and the dark icon on dark branded surfaces when needed.
- Use `logo-mark.svg` for compact product branding, internal empty states, and about/help surfaces.
- Keep the orange accent secondary in product UI. Blue remains the primary interface accent.
